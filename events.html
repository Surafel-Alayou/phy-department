The Ethiopian Maritime Academy at Bahir Dar University graduated 76 candidate sailors trained for the 20th time

Bahir Dar University's Ethiopian Maritime Academy hosted and graduated 76 first-degree mechanical and electrical engineering students from various institutions in Ethiopia. Captain Charl Coetzee, the academy's dean, welcomed guests and emphasized its role in producing qualified seafaring professionals globally. The academy remains a strong institution committed to international standards.
