In the academic year 2015 E.C, Bahir Dar University's College of Natural Sciences warmly welcomes new students.

In the 2015 E.C academic year, the College of Natural Sciences, in collaboration with the College of Social Sciences,

organizes an orientation program for all incoming students in the natural and social sciences at the main campus.

Students with names starting from A to M (inclusive) in the Natural Science field will gather at Camplus,

while those with names from N to Z will assemble at the Grand Hall (Amphi) in the Camplus for Social Science students.

The orientation will take place from the 20th to the 22nd of the month of October 2015 E.C.

For additional information, visit the Registrar's Office website: https://bdu.edu.et/registrar
