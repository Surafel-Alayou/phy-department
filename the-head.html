Message from head

Tamiru Nigussie (PhD)

Dear Students and Colleagues,

I am honored to serve as the head of our Physics Department, a community dedicated to exploring the fundamental mysteries of the universe. Our department is a place of discovery, where faculty and students alike embark on journeys of scientific exploration.

As we continue to push the boundaries of knowledge, I encourage you all to engage in the exciting world of physics with curiosity and enthusiasm. Together, we will unravel the secrets of the universe and make meaningful contributions to the world of science.

Duties & Responsiblities


 Be an assistant professor and above, unless the conditions does not allow.

 Serve as a member of the academic council (AC).

 Follow up and execute the decisions of the DC.

 Serves as chairperson for the department council.

 Form the department graduate council.

 Develope and manage the department's development strategy.

 Participate in the academic unit's strategy development.

 Develope appropriate and relevant academic programs and manage their delivery.

 Develope, manage and renovate the curriculum of the department.

 Communicate academic programs for the Vice Dean.

 Prepare class schedules in consulation with Vice Dean as necessary.

 Provide orientation and induction to the students and staffs of the program.

 Enable students and other customers of the department get-up-date guide books and other relevant supports respectively.

 Conduct continual assesment, evaluation or graduate follow-up and provision of feedback.

 Monitors and gives support to course chairs.

 Ensures the completion of appraisal for all academic staff members.

 Develope and implement a seminar program that provides students with exposure to research.

 Develope a vision that supports the particular goals of the department and broader goals of the academic unit.

 Plan, implement and report departmental t-learning, research and community service activities.

 Promote team sprit for addressing departmental and institutional issues.

 Build necessary relationships with other departments.

 Ensure the validity of necessary resources.

 Develope tools that are aimed at improving student's academic performance and monitor their implementation.

 Address student's academic problems in the courses.

 Assign academic advisors and course chairs.

 Follow up submissions of grades and approve on time to the AU registrar office.

 Establish exam commitee, develope exam bank as necessary to insure quality assesment and evaluation.

 Excecute other tasks given by Dean/Director and Vice Dean.
